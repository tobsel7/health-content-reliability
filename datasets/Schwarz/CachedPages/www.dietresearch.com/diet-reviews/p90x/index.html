P90X Reviewed

There’s no such thing as a magic pill. To lose weight, you have to eat better and exercise more. That’s the foundation of the P90X weight-loss program, which sends you workout materials and DVDs, and a strict nutrition guide.

While the health experts at DietResearch.com agree that nothing comes for free, we believe the price for P90X is too high.

Unless you have a lot of time, a lot of money, and you’re already in shape, this program is not for you.

P90X is eXtremely expensive, difficult

Safety and Quality

The P90X plan is certainly safe. P90X workouts target muscle definition and cardiovascular exercise. The P90X nutrition guide has three phases: a high-protein diet to burn fat, a high-carb diet to boost energy, and a lean diet to enhance endurance.

Effectiveness

With a strict diet and intense workout program, those who stuck with P90X saw results. However, it’s not for everyone. In fact, it’s not for most people.

P90X recommends its product for people who are already in shape but need to tone up or lose less than 15 pounds.

Check out these P90X reviews:
“I am finding it hard to get the full hour in everyday right now. But that is life. You need to make it a priority and that is what I’m doing. I have already dropped 6 pounds in the last few weeks and I feel so much stronger in such a short time.” — Steve
“p90x is an awesome workout, it was a bit tough at the start but now im on day 23. for anyone sitting on the fence, i really do recommend it, even though its a tad bit expensive.” – Kathlan

Cost

The P90X package costs a whopping $119.97 plus shipping for 90 days.

Customer Service

P90X is a product of Beach Body Company, which has B- rating with the Better Business Bureau.

This is a subsidiary of Product Partners, which is not rated. However, consumers have complained of unsolicited charges being made to their credit cards:

“I ORDERED PRODUCT FROM THIS COMPANY THAT SHOULD HAVE BEEN $159.36. THEY TOOK THE LIBERTY OF ENROLLING ME IN ADDITIONAL PROGRAMS AND BILLED MY CREDIT FOR THEM. A PRODUCT THAT STARTED OUT AT $159.36 AND ENDED UP $291.00” – Phyllis S.

“They falsely charged my account twice. And from doing some searching on the internet, it sounds like we are not the first victims.” – Chris C.

Money-back guarantee

P90X has a 90-day money-back guarantee.

Conclusion

With reports of unsolicited credit card charges (from an already expensive program), and extreme commitment demands, DietResearh.com cannot recommend P90X.

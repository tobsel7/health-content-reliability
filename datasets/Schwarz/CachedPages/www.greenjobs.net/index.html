The mission of Green Jobs Network is to connect people seeking jobs that focus on
environmental and social responsibility with available opportunities and resources.


We offer a green job board, green job e-mail list, blog on green collar jobs, and listings of
green career books, green job fairs, green job boards, solar jobs, and jobs at environmental non-profits.

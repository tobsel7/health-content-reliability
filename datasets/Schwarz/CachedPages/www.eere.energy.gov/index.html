DOE Releases Report on Economic Impact of Recovery Act Advanced Vehicle Investments

July 15, 2010 – Ahead of President Obama's trip to Holland, Michigan, DOE released a new report on the economic impact of Recovery Act investments in advanced batteries and vehicles. Full Story.

DOE and DOI Announce Solar Energy Demonstration Projects in Nevada

July 8, 2010 – Secretary Steven Chu, Secretary Ken Salazar, and Senate Majority Leader Harry Reid announced the new Solar Demonstration Zone to demonstrate solar energy technologies. Full story.

Timken Retooling for Wind Industry

With help from a 48C Advanced Manufacturing Tax Credit, the company's South Carolina factory is now producing heavy duty bearings used in wind turbines. View story.

DOE Calls for Input on the National Export Initiative

July 2, 2010 – In response to President Obama's National Export Initiative, DOE is helping devise a strategy for doubling U.S. renewable energy and energy efficiency exports by 2015. Full story.

Tea party may refer to:

U.S. History

  • Boston Tea Party, a direct action protest by colonists in Boston, Massachusetts, against the British government on December 16, 1773
  • Edenton Tea Party, a boycott of British tea by 51 women in Edenton, North Carolina, in 1774
  • Chestertown Tea Party, when colonists in Chestertown, Maryland, dumped British tea into the Chester River on May 23, 1774

U.S. Politics

  • Tea Party movement, a protest movement in the United States that began in 2009
    • Tea Party protests, a series of protests associated with the movement

  • Boston Tea Party (political party), an American political party which espouses a libertarian ideology, founded in 2006

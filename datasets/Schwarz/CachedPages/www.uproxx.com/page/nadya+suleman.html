Nadya Suleman

Nadya Suleman, age 33, gave birth to octuplets in California, on January 26, 2009. The single mother already had six children under the age of seven, all conceived by in vitro fertilization, before conceiving the octuplets in the same way. Suleman's extraordinary conception has been the focus of an ethical debate.

On February 11, 2009, it was reported that Suleman launched a website in order to collect donations for her growing family. (via Mahalo)

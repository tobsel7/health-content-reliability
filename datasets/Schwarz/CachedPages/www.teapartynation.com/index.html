SHARRON ANGLE TO ADDRESS NATIONAL TEA PARTY UNITY CONVENTION!

June 17, 2010 – Nashville, TN – Fresh off her stunning primary  victory, Nevada’s Republican Candidate for Senate Sharron Angle will  be a featured speaker at July’s National Tea Party Unity Convention.

NATIONAL TEA PARTY UNITY CONVENTION ANNOUNCES LOU DOBBS AS HEADLINER!!!

Dobbs to share national perspectives with "independent minded" Tea Party delegates

NASHVILLE, Tennessee (May 14, 2010) – The National Tea Party Convention, today, announced that award winning journalist, TV and radio personality, Lou Dobbs, will join the convention delegates and share his vast insights and perspectives as a keynote speaker. Bringing his political and financial knowledge and commentary to bear, Lou Dobbs will offer the delegates a direct and reasoned defense of American exceptionalism while dealing with issues of trade, jobs, politics and the challenges facing America in the 21st century.

Lou Dobbs stated, ” I have had two great experiences with the Tea Party movement, in New Orleans and in New York at the TeaParty365 Tax Day Tea Party. The crowds were large, boisterous and most importantly, made up of hard working Americans who want to take their country back. I am incredibly excited about the opportunity to address the Tea Party in Las Vegas."

Lou Dobbs brings a true independent voice to Talk Radio, occupying the vital middle ground between the conservative and liberal voices that dominate much of the segment today. Lou anchors 3 nationally syndicated radio programs. The Lou Dobbs show, the Lou Dobbs financial reports and The Lou Dobbs encore weekend edition. It's been a year of first's for Lou "ON THE RADIO" starting with one of Lou's most impressive radio achievements by getting fifty signed radio stations to air his program in the first fifty days on the air, a first in radio syndication history! Dobbs was voted to the top ten list of the Most Important radio talk show host's in America by Talkers magazine (2009).

Judson Philips, Founder and President of Tea Party Nation said, “Lou Dobbs is an electrifying personality who says what's on his mind and backs it up with his knowledge and experience throughout his journalistic career. He is hard hitting and will bring a unique perspective to the convention that will hold people's attention while also challenging their thinking."

Lou Dobbs is the Independent voice in today's largely liberal mainstream media coverage. And his Independent message is hitting the hearts of Americans everywhere. As a former CNN anchor, award-winning journalist, best-selling author and America's leading voice of reason, Lou Dobbs packs a powerful punch. His views influence our leaders, his viewers, and his readers. Lou now brings his point of view to the radio airwaves in a daily, 3-hour program. Lou examines the issues, evokes passion and entertains the audience like no other broadcaster.



JOSEPH FARAH OF WORLD NET DAILY TO ADDRESS ATTENDEES AT NATIONAL TEA PARTY UNITY CONVENTION!!!!



NASHVILLE, Tennessee (May 14, 2010) – The National Tea Party Unity Convention is honored to announce Joseph Farah, chairman, CEO and editor-in-chief of WorldNetDaily, will be a headlining speaker at the July event in Las Vegas.

Farah, a nationally syndicated columnist and noted author of several books, including "Taking America Back: A Radical Plan to Revive Freedom, Morality, and Justice," will emphasize the importance of unity within the Tea Party movement. Farah brings a spirited, insightful, and sometimes controversial viewpoint to the political dialogue of the day.

“I have known Joseph Farah for some time and have always enjoyed our repartee," said Judson Phillips, Founder and President of Tea Party Nation. "His message of unity within the Tea Party movement will be well-received. I am grateful that he chose to participate in this convention."

Farah's fearless perspective stems from his role as a government watchdog with more than 30 years of experience in journalism. He spoke at the February convention and is looking to reprise his role by challenging delegates to band together and overcome the challenges of the left, and in particular, the Obama administration.
His insight and keen instinct as an author and intrepid journalist will enliven debate and provide food for thought to convention delegates. Often challenged, never intimidated, Farah will offer a bold message of unity while also recognizing the challenges of this administration and its leftist agenda.


Michael Patrick Leahy of the Nationwide Tea Party Coalition commented, "Joseph Farah has been a strong voice in the conservative movement. His focus on a message of unity in the Tea Party movement is amplified by his stature as Editor-in-Chief of WorldNetDaily and his understanding of issues dividing America today. I look forward to his reasoned and measured discussion of this important topic."

The convention will feature well-known speakers, training sessions on practical skills, such as fundraising, organizing, countering the left’s racism charges and finding conservative voters. The convention will also feature the first “Tea Party Nation Conservative Film Festival,” which will showcase and honor conservative filmmakers and conservative films. Saturday evening we will host a Traditional American Concert and Symposium.

The Tea Party Unity Convention will be held July 15-17 at the Palazzo Las Vegas Resort and Convention Center in Las Vegas, Nevada. The purpose of the convention is to be a unifying event for the Tea Party movement in the run-up to the fall congressional elections.


ANDREW BREITBART TO BE FEATURED SPEAKER AT CONVENTION!

NASHVILLE, Tennessee (May 14, 2010) ␣ Leading conservative and new media entrepreneur, Andrew Breitbart will be headlining at the National Tea Party Convention in Las Vegas. Breitbart agreed to speak at the convention after his electrifying commentary at the Nashville event in February.

Andrew Breitbart is best known for his significant contributions through his new media web empire which includes Breitbart TV, Big Government, Big Journalism and Big Hollywood.

Recently, Breitbart distinguished himself by offering a $100,000 scholarship to the United Negro College Fund upon proof of racially charged language being lobbed at the Congressional Black Caucus during the March "Code Red" Healthcare rallies. Breitbart is an outspoken supporter of the patriots that represent the Tea Party movement and will electrify the expected two thousand delegates.

Breitbart stated, "I want to go to the hottest place in the world in the peak of the Summer to share common cause with new and old Tea Party patriots alike. Las Vegas in July! Exactly! The Tea Party movement exploded on to the scene with a blazing sense of what it needed to do: Get back to basics. The Constitution. Government accountability. Humility. Patriotism. E Pluribus Unum."

He further commented , "We cannot allow the cynics, elitists and the liars to define us. We are the American people and we are coming together to form a united front to take on corruption inside the government and out. That means you in the so-called mainstream media! Ignore the Tea Party and we will grow. Slander us and we will defend ourselves -- and grow, too. We have already walked through fire simply to express our God-given rights to redress our government. Something is wrong when an out of control government finds common cause with the Fourth Estate. In July in Las Vegas the Tea Party movement will show itself to be a 21st century force: united, media savvy, prepared to fight new media wars all in the name of informing an awakening electorate. Our cause is righteous. And in November, if we unify and grow, we will be victorious."

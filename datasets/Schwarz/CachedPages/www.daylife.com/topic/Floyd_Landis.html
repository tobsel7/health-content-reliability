About Floyd Landis

Wikipedia

Floyd Landis (born October 14, 1975) is an American cyclist, currently suspended from competition, who was stripped of overall victory in the 2006 Tour de France for a doping offense after testing revealed a skewed testosterone/epitestosterone ratio. He is a time-trial specialist as well as a strong climber. Full Article

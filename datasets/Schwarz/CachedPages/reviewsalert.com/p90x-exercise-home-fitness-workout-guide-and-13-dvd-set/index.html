P90X Exercise Home Fitness Workout Guide and 13 DVD Set

P90X Exercise Home Fitness Guide and 13 DVD Set

With the P90X workout you get an advanced workout and diet system that’s been proven to take off extra weight and promote muscle growth.

The primary “secret” behind the system is the p90x workout schedule which utilizes an advanced exercise technique called “Muscle Confusion.” You can drastically accelerate your training results by continually introducing new moves and routines into your P90x workouts.

Exercising using muscle confusion means your body never gets  used to your routines, or plateaus. In addition, by constantly adding new moves and routines you never get bored.

There’s a huge amount of variety available to you with this system. So regardless of whether your goals are to tone up, build ripped muscle mass or just lose weight, you’ll find many different exercises to compliment your priorities.

The system is set up to give you a thorough 90 days of intensity. P90X results can begin showing up in as little as a few days thanks in part to a comprehensive 3-phase nutrition plan, detailed fitness guide, and muscle-pumping workouts.

One of the biggest questions I hear is, “Does P90X work?”

Undoubtedly it does, do to the countless amount of people who have said so. P90x is currently the leading workout program on Amazon with tons of positive reviews about its effectiveness. There’s also been many p90x before and after pictures submitted to the user site, showing the transformational effect users have had.

Disc 11: Cardio X – Shred fat with this high energy fat burning workout DVD.

Disc 12: Ab Ripper X - Get the six-pack abs you’ve always wanted with these abdominal exercise routines.

Disc 13: How to Bring It – get an overview of the complete P90X Extreme Home Fitness training system and what to expect.

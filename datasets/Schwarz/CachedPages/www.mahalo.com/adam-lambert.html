  • American Music Award Performance

    Adam's debut performance on the American Music Awards on 11/22/09 shocked America when he and a fellow dancer simulated oral sex, kissed his male keyboardist and grabbed the crotch of a female dancer. ABC received 1500 complaints after the performance due to the racy content and ABC later canceled his performance on Good Morning America because they were worried about similar controversial antics from Lambert. CBS' The Early Show quickly snatched him up to sing 2 songs from his new album "For Your Entertainment" and to address the controversy from the AMAs. However CBS has been since criticized for blurring Adam's kiss with his male keyboardist but not blurring a similar kiss from the VMAs with Madonna, Britney Spears and Christina Aguilera. Viewers are claiming that CBS is guilty of the same double standard that Adam was speaking about in the interview. Adam's debut cd sales have soared since his AMA performance and many are saying that this controversy will end up helping sales by keeping him on everyone's mind during the first few weeks of the release.

  • AT&T and Idol

    On May 26, 2009, The New York Times reported that AT&T swayed fans to vote for Kris Allen. Sources claim AT&T provided phones with free text-messaging and even gave lessons in how to power vote at parties in Arkansas for Allen. No similar attempts were made for runner-up Lambert.NYT: AT&T May Have Swayed ‘Idol’ Results (May 26, 2009)

  • Spider Man

    MTV.com reported on April 14, 2009, that Lambert was up for the lead role in the Broadway Spider-Man Musical titled Turn Off The Dark. However, Lambert was unable to audition for the role as he was already involved in American Idol. Casting director Bernie Telsey stated that he'd "gladly consider Lambert for a bunch of shows" in the future "especially now that he has national name recognition."MTV: Adam Lambert Was Up For Lead In Spider-Man Musical (April 14, 2009)

  • The Kisses

    In the first week of March 2009, photos surfaced on the Internet of Adam Lambert kissing another man, known only as "Brad." While Brad won't make an official comment, the photos could be seen on his MySpace page with he and Lambert in several different intimate poses.Radar Online: Introducing Adam Lambert's Mystery Man (March 6, 2009) MySpace: The Boy From Venus Some critics have expressed concern that the photos may negatively affect Lambert's experience on American Idol, while others believe the photos could benefit him by thrusting him into the limelight even further.

    The photos were discussed a month later on The O'Reilly Factor, where the consensus was that his sexual orientation should have nothing to do with the show, only his talent.Vote For The Worst: Adam Lambert Pics on Bill O'Reilly (April 7, 2009)

  • Adam Lambert Gay?

    American Idol judge Kara DioGuardi spoke about Lambert's sexuality on The View in late May 2009 and said she thinks he has been "always openly out [of the closet]." On June 1, 2009, Lambert was spotted holding the hand of interior designer Drake LaBry as they left a nightclub together in West Hollywood.OK!: Adam Lambert Debuts Boyfriend (June 2, 2009) Lambert, who has never discussed his sexuality in public, is expected to "officially come out" on the cover of Rolling Stone magazine in June 2009.New Your Post: Come Out

    Lambert admitted to being gay in the June 2009 issue of Rolling Stone where he stated, "I dont think it should be a surprise for anyone to hear that Im gay." Lambert also admitted to being a bit distracted with having to room with Kris Allen during his stay on Idol because Lambert had a crush on Allen.http://latimesblogs.latimes.com/thedishrag/2009/06/idol-winner-chris-allen-calls-adam-lambert-and-drake-labry-a-cool-combination-.html

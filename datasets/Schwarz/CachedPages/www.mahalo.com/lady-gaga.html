  • This page is about the performer and song writer Lady GaGa. She was born Stefani Joanne Angelina Germanotta and she now goes by the name Lady GaGa which is from the Queen song "Radio Ga-Ga".

    Lady GaGa is a Dance-Pop/Electronica artist who released her debut album, The Fame, in 2008. Her career has taken off and skyrocketed since that first release.


    She was nominated for a Grammy Award for Best Dance Recording for her song "Just Dance."Grammy.com: 51st annual Grammy Nominations List


    On March 19, 2010, reports surfaced that GaGa was being sued by her former producing partner and ex-boyfriend Rob Fusari.http://www.kansascity.com/2010/03/18/1822142/producer-files-35m-suit-against.html Fusari is seeking $30.5 million in damages saying that he co-wrote Paparazzi and Beautiful, Dirty, Rich, created her stage name for her and helped her land a record deal.

    Fusari has received checks for royalties but he did not negotiate the last one due to the wording on the check. It has "endorsed in accord and satisfaction of all sums due to undersigned" written on the back of the check and Fusari feels it is a ploy to cease paying him royalties.http://www.mtv.com/news/articles/1634292/20100319/lady_gaga.jhtml

Cash For Clunkers - Minivans

by Vehix.com Sunday, July 26, 2009

The Minivans on our list meet the following eligibility requirements for the “Cash for Clunkers” program:

  • An average fuel economy of 18 MPG or less
  • An average trade-in value of less than $3,500 - $4,500 (NOTE: vehicle values can vary dramatically due to condition, mileage, style and trim. You can confirm used vehicle values according to NADA Guides)

We’ve also included qualifying minivans that meet the “Cash for Clunkers” requirements that you might consider as a replacement. This list is not exhaustive and does not represent all eligible vehicles but should help you look for a replacement ride.

New replacement minivans with mileage of at least 18 mpg are eligible for vouchers. If the mileage of the new minivan is at least 2 mpg higher than the old vehicle, the voucher is worth $3,500. If the mileage of the new minivan is at least 5 mpg higher than the old vehicle, the voucher is worth $4,500.

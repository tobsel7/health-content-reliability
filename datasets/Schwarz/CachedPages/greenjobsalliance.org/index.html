Our Purpose

The Green Jobs Alliance was established in direct response to the Green Jobs Act. With millions of Americans out of work, the Alliance is presented with a unique opportunity to assist in assembling an education and training infrastructure that will create jobs in our economy, help our citizens become more energy efficient, and protect our environment—all at the same time.

Through a collaborative effort with our partners, we are laying the foundation for establishing one of America’s first nationwide nonprofit partnerships required by the Green Jobs Act to provide education, training and job placement to thousands of Americans.  We hope to complement the work of organizations such as Green for All, the Apollo Alliance and the Blue-Green Alliance, and become the regional prototype for how nonprofit partnerships can be organized to implement the Act throughout the Nation.

To provide education and training, the Alliance has brought together an extensive and
tested network of existing training partners. These training partners, organized by
Labor and Management, include:

  • Labor organizations, Employer Associations, and
    Joint Labor-Management Training Programs
  • Environmental companies
  • Workforce Investment Boards
  • Educational Institutions and Technical Schools
  • Pathways Out of Poverty training schools
  • Environmental organizations
  • Community-based groups
  • Veteran’s service organizations

As our Alliance continues to grow, please feel free to contact us at 757-788-9073 or at info@greenjobsalliance.org.  It’s not too late to join the Green Revolution!  In fact, it’s just begun!

Renewable Energy

Solar Electric Roadmap for Arizona
In January, 2007 the Arizona Department of Commerce released the state’s Solar Roadmap Study which projects up to 1,000 megawatts of solar electricity could be implemented in Arizona by 2020 creating more than 3,000 new jobs in the process and reducing emissions by 400,000 tons per year.

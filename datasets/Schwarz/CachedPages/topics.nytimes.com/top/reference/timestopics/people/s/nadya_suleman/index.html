Nadya Suleman

NBC

Nadya Suleman gave birth to octuplets Jan. 26, 2009, in Bellflower, Calif.

She previously had six children, ranging in age from 2 to 7. All 14 were conceived through vitro fertilization, the last eight using frozen embryos from previous pregnancies, Ms. Suleman’s mother said.

Ms. Suleman, 33, earned a degree in child and adolescent development in 2006 from California State University at Fullerton and in 2008 was studying for a master’s degree in counseling, according to a university spokeswoman interviewed in The Long Beach Post-Telegram.

Her mother, Angela Suleman, said her daughter has been obsessed with having children since she was a teenager.

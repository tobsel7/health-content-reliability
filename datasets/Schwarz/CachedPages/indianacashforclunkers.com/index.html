Cash For Clunkers Program
The US Government has created an Automotive Stimulus package that gives consumers up to $4500 vouchers for the purchase of a new vehicle.

Customers can trade-in a vehicle getting up to $4500 from the governments automotive stimulus program called “Cash for Clunkers” - a program that allows consumers with older, less fuel efficient vehicles to trade in their "clunker" for a voucher worth up to $4,500 toward the purchase or lease of a new car with improved gas mileage of between 2 and 10 miles per gallon, depending on the vehicle purchased.

To qualify for the money your trade-in "clunker" car would have to meet a couple of requirements.  To find out the amount of the voucher you qualify for complete the form  to the left .As the details of the program are being updated, some changes are expected.

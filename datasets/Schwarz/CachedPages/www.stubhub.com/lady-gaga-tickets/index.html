Lady Gaga Tickets - Musical Theatrics!

Lady Gaga, born Stephanie Germanotta in Manhattan, New York, is a dance-pop singer, songwriter and pianist who started performing in the New York underground scene as a teen. A job working with Interscope Records as a songwriter led to collaborations with the Pussycat Dolls. When her solo work started to hit the airwaves, it was the single "Just Dance" that put her on the map. Her 2008 debut album, The Fame, shot up the charts.

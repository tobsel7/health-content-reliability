Cash for Clunkers - Now Officially Available
July 24, 2009 - CARS (Cash for Clunkers) is now officially available to the public! The Final Rules and Terms of Eligibility have been published on cars.gov (click here to view a copy). Dealers are now evaluating trades for eligibility. It is strongly recommended that you act now to ensure funds are available. It is estimated that the initial funding will run out in less than a few weeks.
Important Things to Know - (from http://cars.gov)
Your vehicle must be less than 25 years old on the trade-in date
Only purchase or lease of new vehicles qualify
You don't need a voucher, dealers will apply a credit at purchase
Generally, trade-in vehicles must get 18 or less MPG (some very large pick-up trucks and cargo vans have different requirements)
Trade-in vehicles must be registered and insured continuously for the full year preceding the trade-in

Mortgage Refinancing

Find mortgage refinance choices to improve your financial flexibility

If you have a fixed-rate mortgage, it can be frustrating when the latest interest rates are lower than the one on your current loan. It’s possible to take advantage of lower interest rates and effectively reduce your monthly payments through mortgage refinancing. At Nationwide Bank®, we help you pay less on what you borrow through conventional mortgage refinance options to suit your specific needs.

Refinancing your home may also enable you to use the equity that you've accrued for things like funding home improvements, saving for your children’s education or consolidating debt. When you refinance a mortgage loan, it may also allow you to shorten the term of your mortgage or replace one type of mortgage with another. By taking advantage of traditional mortgage refinancing, you can enjoy increased financial flexibility and comfortably budget all of life’s expenses.

Start saving with mortgage refinancing. Begin your mortgage application today!

Attractive mortgage refinance rates to fit your budget

At Nationwide Bank, we offer attractive mortgage refinance rates to help reduce your monthly loan payments. To assist you in determining if this is a good time to refinance your mortgage loan, check out our great rates to see how they work with your budget. Another way we are On Your Side is with the Best Price Guarantee. As a lower-cost mortgage lender, we guarantee an attractive price on your mortgage choice or we’ll pay you $300.

Our process is simple, convenient and safe.

Learn how to refinance a mortgage loan to get the rate you want

It’s important to have a good understanding of your credit history and credit score before applying to refinance your mortgage loan. The better your score, the better the interest rate you may be eligible for – and the more money you may be able to save. Making smarter decisions with your money is easy with the traditional mortgage refinancing options available to you through Nationwide Bank.

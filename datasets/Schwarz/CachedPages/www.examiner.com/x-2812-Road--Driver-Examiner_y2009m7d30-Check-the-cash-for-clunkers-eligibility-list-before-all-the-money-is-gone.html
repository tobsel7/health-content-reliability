cash for clunkers eligibility list is best way to see of you qualify

The cash for clunkers money is going going and will soon be gone, so if you your are interested in taking advantage of the program you better check the complete cash for clunkers eligibility list to see if your clunker qualifies.

The most recent and complete list of cash for clunkers eligible vehicles is HERE.

According to the National Highway Traffic Safety Administration, the Federal agency that administers the program dealers had submitted requests for reimbursement for vouchers of up to $4,500 on 22,782 vehicles, for a total of $96 million so far.

That means that in just the first couple days of the program a total of 10 percent of the one billion dollars allocated to scrapping clunkers has already been spent by the Fed.

It appears that these funds could actually run out in a matter of weeks," said U.S. Rep. Candice Miller.

"Having this program last for only a few weeks would limit this program's potential and disenfranchise a number of consumers who wish to take advantage of the program by turning in their inefficient vehicle for a new, more fuel efficient model," the Michigan Congresswomen wrote. "This program has the possibility to truly jumpstart our economy"

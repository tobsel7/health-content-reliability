Do you have a vehicle, generator, boat, tractor, or any engine for that matter that runs on diesel? You could be using biodiesel that can be made at home for as little as 0.75 cents a gallon.

Drive down the road and know that the fuel you are burning is fuel that YOU made at HOME.

Biodiesel is a high quality fuel made through a process called transesterification. Don’t let that big word scare you, because the process is relatively simple once you’ve practiced it a couple of times. Many have learned that they can successfully make your their own biodiesel fuel right in their own backyard. All you need is the right equipment, and the right information to properly understand the the process.

Never go to a fuel station again.

The lubricity of biodiesel is far greater than petroleum diesel.

Biodiesel has a much higher flashpoint making it much less likely to ignite in a car wreck

Biodiesel reduces engine wear by as much as one half.

Unlike petroleum diesel, it’s made from renewable resources such as corn & soybean.

Biodiesel is non-toxic and biodegradable.

Production & use of biodiesel results in a 78.5% reduction of carbon dioxide emissions.

Pleasant smelling, both in its raw form, and when burned.

Your engine will run much quieter.

Biodiesel reduces our dependence on non-renewable oils, which threaten our planet with climate change.

It will work for you and it will work for anyone in the world that wants to learn these steps. Once you get started using these newly discovered techniques, you will only question why it took you so long to hear about this insanely simple solution. Discover the joy of making your own fuel and never go to a gas station again.

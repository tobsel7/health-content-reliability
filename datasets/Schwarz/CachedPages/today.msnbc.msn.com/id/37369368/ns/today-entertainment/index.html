David Cook, Adam Lambert missing on ‘Idol’

Former winner had charity event; season 8 runner-up put on vocal rest

Advertisement | ad info
Matt Sayles / AP
Former "Idol" winner David Cook missed out on the finale and Simon Cowell's farewell.
Access Hollywood
updated 5/26/2010 10:50:06 PM ET
  • Share
  • Print
  • Font:
  • +
  • -

LOS ANGELES — Every winner of “American Idol” appeared on the show’s stage on Wednesday night to help the show bid adieu to departing judge Simon Cowell — except for one, season seven winner David Cook.

Access Hollywood has learned that Cook’s absence, however, was nothing dramatic.

Video: Adam Lambert on Simon Cowell

Cook had a charity event in Kansas City, his rep told Access Hollywood of the former “Idol” champ, who is known for his support of charities — in particular those related to cancer, a disease that stripped him of his brother.

Photo gallery: David Cook: From ‘Idol’ to rock star

Another popular “Idol” alumnus who was also conspicuously missing from the finale was Adam Lambert.

Lambert’s rep said that the Season 8 runner-up did not make it to the show as he was put on vocal rest.

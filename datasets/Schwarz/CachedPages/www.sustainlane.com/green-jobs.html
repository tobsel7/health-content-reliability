It’s time to pop your green collar. Green jobs are all the rage, and as the United States transitions to a green economy, green jobs are in demand. You may have picked up a copy of activist and environmentalist Van Jones’ acclaimed book The Green Collar Economy: How One Solution Can Fix Our Two Biggest Problems, and decided that you want to snag one of the many green jobs to become part of the burgeoning green collar workforce; maybe you heard Obama’s pledge to create a green economy. Thousands of green jobs will need to be filled by green collar workers, and SustainLane’s green jobs board has over 580 listings and is growing each day. A career in the green sector is a recession-proof place in our troubled economy, and SustainLane is here to guide you through listings of green jobs across the nation.

Think you have to have a fancy environmental degree to get a green job? Think again. Green jobs exist in all fields, from finance to construction to writing—and everything else in between.

If you’re just returning to the job search after years of gainful employment, or if you’re one of the thousands of recent grads entering the job market during a recession, or if you’re just looking for a green collar career change, SustainLane offers you tools on how to effectively promote yourself in today’s competitive green jobs market. Resume building strategies and advice on how to build your green networking skills are all available on SustainLane. Finding and applying for green jobs is a cinch on SustainLane.com.

SustainLane’s green jobs board is a place where employers, recruiters, and staffers can post green jobs, and green jobs seekers can look in one convenient place for green collar work all across the country. And get this: it’s all free.

SustainLane is a site that connects interested consumers with the tools and information on everything related to green, including businesses, products, consumer-generated how-tos, news, product reviews of new green offerings in the marketplace, and, of course green jobs. If you are an employer, recruiter of staffer looking to find enthusiastic green jobs seekers, SustainLane’s community of all-things-green enthusiasts is an ideal place to post an ad to help hire your next green collar employee of the month.

Get on the green jobs bandwagon. Become one of the green collar wearing members of today’s workforce. Join the green economy. Make SustainLane your go-to destination for green jobs, information on the green economy, and all the tools you need to don a green collar successfully.

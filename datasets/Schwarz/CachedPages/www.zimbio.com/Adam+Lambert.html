Adam Lambert was a contestant on the eighth season of American Idol. Lambert sang "Bohemian Rhapsody" for his initial audition in San Francisco, California. He finished the season as the runner-up to Kris Allen.
He has released one single entitled "No Boundaries" and is reported to be working on his first album.

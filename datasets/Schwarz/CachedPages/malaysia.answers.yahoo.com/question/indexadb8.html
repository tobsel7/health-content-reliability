    I would only buy and hold physical gold. I would not buy it in a mutual fund or as any sort of gold stocks. The problem with buying a gold fund is that the Federal government could step in any day out of desperation and pass a law making it illegal to own gold in any form. If you have a gold-backed IRA or any gold-backed stock fund, these would simply disappear and you would be left with nothing, or else worthless paper stocks. The only way to own gold (or silver) in my opinion is to buy and take possession under your own control of gold bullion or gold coins. This means to have the gold shipped to you and then you hide it someplace (not in a bank), like burying it in the ground someplace, hiding it in your heating ducts, or plastering it into the wall.

    If the government knows who owns gold or can get its hands on it, you can bet they will.

    Gold has an intrinsic value, as do silver and platinum, other common investment vehicles. You could carry $100,000 of value in a few platinum coins in your pocket. By comparison, the paper US dollar has an intrinsic value of exactly nothing.

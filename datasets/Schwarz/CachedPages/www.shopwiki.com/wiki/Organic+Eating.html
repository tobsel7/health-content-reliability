Organic Eating

Organic eating  has become popular in recent years mainly as a consequence of pesticides, chemicals, and hormones being used on produce and animals over the past century. Organic foods  simply do not contain any of these unnatural chemicals, but follow certain standards of farming and gardening which make them organic certified . This means a food which is labeled "organic" has been grown, or made of ingredients which have been grown, on soil that has been free of chemicals for a number of years.

Health Benefits
  • The main health benefits  of eating organic foods would be avoiding the pesticides used on produce. Pesticides and insecticides are also harmful for the environments and also affect many marine populations. 
  • Research shows that organic milk  contains more antioxidants , omega 3, and other vitamins -- all of which are very beneficial.
  • Much organic food  is grown locally which means freshness at the market and better taste.

Foods

Organic Fruits 

Apples, pears, and strawberries are the fruits most likely to have pesticides, so it's best to keep an eye out for the organic label if you eat these fruits regularly.

Organic Vegetables 

Pesticides are also commonly found on lettuce, carrots, and celery; so, again, buying these from an organic produce  farm or market is the safest choice.

Organic Grains 

Buying products of organic whole grains or wheat is great for people who love cereals ,oatmeal ,bread , or pasta  --without the extra chemicals!

Organic Meats 

Animals being bred for the organic meat market are very closely monitored; they generally do not get hormone injections and they feed off of only organic grains and vegetation.

Organic Beverages 

Organic drinks  have become popular in recent years with more availability in local supermarkets and grocery stores. Parents also appreciate drinks for kids with less sugar and added chemicals.

Snacks

Organic Desserts 

It is very possible to have a completely organic diet  which does not have to rule out desserts . As long as all the ingredients which make up the organic ice cream ,organic chocolate , etc. are made from organic products  and are organic certified, you are good to go.

Organic Snacking 

Organic snacks like granola bars ,honey bars ,vegetable chips ,trail mix , etc. are great to always have in stock when you get the munchies, but don't want to veer off into non-organic eating.

Floyd Landis

subscribe
Highlights

Floyd Landis is a pro cyclist who is from near the Lehigh Valley area. Landis grew up in nearby Farmersville in West Earl Township, Lancaster County, Pennsylvania. The cyclist is best known for his 2006 Tour de France title -- which he was later stripped of -- after his involvement in a major doping scandal in 2006. The result was a two-year ban from pro racing.  Show more »

    I am 21 years old and I was given the H1N1 vaccine. 10 hours after getting it I got severe chills, muscle aches, head ache, and a 103.4 fever. It lasted about 2 days and then just went away. The doctors thought (or wanted to think) that I had just coincidentally gotten the flu at the same time as getting the vaccine, but I'm not so sure about that. I did not have any coughing or nasal congestion. It seemed to have just lowered my immune system significantly and my body reacted very severely to the vaccine. I was given the injectible vaccine because I have asthma, I was told the nasal one has even more side effects. I wouldn't suggest the vaccine for you.

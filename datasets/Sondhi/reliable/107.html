Associated Therapists, Inc.

Associated Therapists, Inc. provides administrative support to a group of multi-disciplinary independent Mental Health private practitioners located in Southern California.


Professional Staff

The professionals include: Psychiatrists, Clinical Psychologists, Nurse Practitioners, Licensed Clinical Social Workers, Marriage, Family and Child Counselors, possessing a variety of specialties and orientations. Each person has a web page describing some personal and professional information regarding their education, experience and areas of specialty.

Voice mail phone numbers for each therapist are included in these web pages.

 

Forms and Questionnaires

Forms and questionnaires that you can use to help report information about yourself and or your child. You may use these on or before your first visit as well as during the course of treatment. These forms must be downloaded and then printed on your computer. You may then fill them out and bring them or mail them to your therapist, nurse or doctor.

 

Locations - Maps

Services Offered

The professionals at Associated Therapists, Inc. offer a wide range of individual, couples, group and family therapy. Since the individual practitioners have a differing range of professional interests and education they will evaluate and treat many types of disorders and problems. In addition, specialized programs are offered for many different problem areas.

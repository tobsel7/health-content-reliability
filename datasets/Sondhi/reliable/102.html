#1 AHUN
Adjustable Spray Nasal-Throat Irrigation Instrument Price: $21.00+ S/H

The effective feature of this design is the Nasal Tip, which permits for an ADJUSTABLE Nasal-Throat Spray irrigation.
The performance of the Cat.#AHUN's Tip is one whereby liquid solution, entering one nostril, exits the other. This leaves a refreshing and clear passage for breathing more freely and the comfort that this affords. The cilia are invigorated and function responsibly.
Throat infections are readily treated with liquid solutions recommended by the Physician.

#2 NFT
Nasal-Throat Flush Irrigation Instrument Price: $21.00 + S/H

This product is designed to produce a steady liquid solution stream under finite volume and intensity control to accommodate any tissue sensitivity. The liquid stream enters and exits the same nostril independently. While extremely effective for the hygiene of the sinus pockets, it is most effective in the flushing of deep crypts of the throat. Gargling is only moderately effective.
The spraying of medicated liquid solutions on directly targeted areas with the Cat.# NFT is the proven means for the relief of most any throat ailment.

Combination Adjustable Spray Nasal-Throat Irrigator and Nasal-Throat Flush Irrigator Price: $26.00 + S/H

This product is a combination of the Cat.# AHUN and the Cat.# NFT - both of which are depicted in the above presentations. The purpose is to enable the patient to select and alternate nasal - throat hygiene Tips for the most effective treatment.

#4 PL-NF-TU

A Flexible Flush Irrigator designed to provide a steady liquid solution flow near the sinus pockets, as originally designed for the NIH by ethicare. Now available in our general product line. Similar to the NFT, but is soft and flexible for deeper irrigation.

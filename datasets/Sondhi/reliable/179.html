About Healthnotes

Headquartered in Portland, Oregon, Healthnotes, Inc. is the premier provider of reliable, easy-to-use health, food, and lifestyle information for Web sites and interactive touchscreen kiosks.

Used by leading supermarkets, pharmacies, and health food stores in the United States, Canada, and the United Kingdom, Healthnotes Retail Solutions empower consumers to make educated decisions and drive product sales-online and in-store. HNI also generates Web applications that are licensed to e-commerce and health-related Internet sites, as well as reference tools for physicians and healthcare professionals.

Our mission

Healthnotes is your in-store and online guide to healthy living. Brought to you by your favorite retailers, Healthnotes reliable science-based content gives you the current, relevant, trustworthy information you need to choose the best products to support your lifestyle. Take control of your health, save time, and save money with Healthnotes.

20 years' experience

Healthnotes expertise in content creation, delivery, and management can help develop your kiosk programs and Web initiatives into dynamic, engaging customer experiences in-store and online.

Editorial process

Incorporating research gathered from scientific studies published in over 600 medical and scientific journals, continuous updating of Healthnotes content is overseen by Alan R. Gaby, MD, Chief Medical Editor. For details on our authors and editorial process, please see Healthnotes Authors and Contributors.

Healthnotes management team has extensive experience in medical publishing, education, healthcare, consumer marketing, retail, and technology. Before joining Healthnotes, Inc., team members made contributions to companies such as Oracle, Johnson & Johnson, Intel, WebMD, GNC, Qwest, Dex, 3M, Mattel, Corbis, Webtrends, iAnywhere, RealNetworks, and Retek, to name a few. In addition to their experience and knowledge, our management team shares the common goal of building Healthnotes, Inc. into an exceptional company.

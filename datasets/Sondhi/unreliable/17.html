Alternative Cures For Headaches

People who suffer from headaches will do just about anything to make the pain of the headaches go away. Headaches plague most people at one time or another and are one of the primary reasons why people end up missing work. If you suffer from headaches, you are one of millions looking for a cure.

  

There are several treatments for headaches, a common malady, but no cures. Those who are prone to headaches will continue to get them. Some of the most common causes of headaches are:

Hormone fluctuation (this is when a woman will get headaches around the time of her periods)

Stress

Allergies - usually related to some sort of food

Sinus allergy headaches

Migraine headaches (no known cause)

Eye strain

High blood pressure

Contrary to what you may believe, brain tumors are not the likely cause of headaches. As a matter of fact, unless the headache is so severe that the person passes out or cannot get up out of bed, a brain tumor will not be considered as a cause for headaches. In most cases, headaches are nothing to worry about. The only time you have to worry about headaches is if you have a sudden and severe headache that will not subside. This can be the symptom of a burst blood vessel in the brain and needs medical attention.

In most cases, you will never know the reason for your headaches, especially if you are unlucky enough to suffer from migraine headaches. Although some doctors believe that migraine headaches are the result of allergies, there is no medical evidence supporting this theory.

Some natural remedies for headaches work just as well as medications without the harmful side effects. One herbal cure for headaches is lavender. This can be purchased as an essential oil and can be rubbed on the head to alleviate the symptoms of headaches.

Other alternative remedies for headaches include acupuncture and massage treatments. Both of these have proven to be effective at curing headaches. If your headache is caused by stress, you may try St. John's Wort as an alternative cure for stress caused headaches.

Headaches can be very problematic but are usually not a sign of serious illness. Other natural remedies for headaches include an icepack on the head. This can help relive some of the pain. Still yet another alternative and unusual cure for headaches is to drink cola out of an aluminum can with a straw. Something about the caffeine in the cola reacting with the aluminum is supposed to cure headaches.

When you are talking about alternative cures for headaches, be advised that what works for one person may not work for another. Try different alternative cures to help your headaches and you may find that you come across the perfect cure.

Home Remedies for Controlling Diabetes Mellitus

Diabetes mellitus is considered to be one of the most lethal of the lifestyle diseases today. It is killing a large number of the world population every year, and the number is only going up each year. Awareness and modification in the lifestyle seem to be the only way to control this silent monster, but there is very less effort going on in that area.

Diabetes mellitus is a condition of hyperglycemia, i.e. a condition when the blood sugar level increases. The beta cells in the islets of Langerhans in the human pancreas secrete a hormone known as the insulin. This insulin is responsible for the proper utilization of sugars, especially glucose, in the human body. In case the insulin is deficient, the sugars will not be able to be assimilated properly and they will be retained in the bloodstream. This is the condition known as diabetes mellitus.

There are three main types of diabetes mellitus. They are:-

(i) Diabetes Mellitus Type 1 – This is the Insulin Dependent Diabetes Mellitus, which occurs in young people and children. For this reason, it is also known as juvenile diabetes. In this type of diabetes, the person has a total absence of the insulin hormone in the body.

(ii) Diabetes Mellitus Type 2 – This is the Non Insulin Dependent Diabetes Mellitus, which occurs generally in people of the middle age. In this type of diabetes mellitus, the person's body does produce insulin, but it is not enough to meet the requirement of the body, or else, the diabetes is not able to be utilized in the blood properly.

(iii) Gestational Diabetes Mellitus – This type of diabetes mellitus occurs during pregnancy in women, when there are several hormonal changes occurring in the body. This is a temporary type of diabetes, and it usually corrects itself after childbirth.

There could be some other causes for diabetes mellitus, such as a disease of the pancreas, malnutrition, hormonal imbalances, etc. Factors such as the age of the person, gender, diet and nutrition, active or sedentary lifestyle, etc. play a very important role in influencing the blood sugar levels of the body.

Home Remedies for Diabetes Mellitus

One most important thing to be remembered is that there is no definitely cure for diabetes mellitus. This is a disease that lasts for life. However, attempts can be made to keep the amount of blood sugar in check. If that is done, then a person with diabetes mellitus can also lead an almost normal healthy and long life.

Another thing to note is that none of the remedies given below can work by themselves in treating diabetes mellitus. No treatment is possible without proper dietary control. The diet should be certainly low in carbohydrates and fats, but it should also be nutritious at the same time.

Read the following home remedies. They are good at bringing the blood sugar level down. But most importantly, these remedies are safe to try out for a diabetic patient, without any side-effects.

The bitter melon (karela, Momordica charantia) is one of the best home remedies for diabetes mellitus. For three months continuously, prepare a dish of the bitter melon in ghee. Have this dish daily. This will definitely help in bringing the blood sugar to a manageable level.

Fenugreek (Trigonella foenum graecum) is another greatly beneficial remedy for controlling blood sugar. Every morning have a cupful of the juice of the fenugreek leaves and then chew the hard seeds of the herb. This will gradually reduce the blood sugar and will help to bring the body back to normal. This is one of the good diabetes mellitus home remedies.

Every morning have tomatoes, oranges and jamuns (Indian plum, Syzygium cumini). The total amount of these three should come to 300 grams per day. This will certainly bring the blood sugar down if done diligently and on a regular basis.

Soak some black raisins in water and keep them overnight. When you wake up in the morning, strain this solution. Drink the clear water that is obtained. This is a wonderful remedy for controlling the blood sugar. Do it on a regular basis. This is one of the useful diabetes mellitus treatment.

One of the best remedies for controlling diabetes mellitus is definitely the amalaki (Indian gooseberry, Emblica officinalis). Soak a single gooseberry in a glassful of water and let it remain for some time. Then filter this water, and squeeze the juice of a single lemon in the water. Drink this every morning without fail. This is one of the best remedies to get rid of the excessive amount of sugar in the blood. This is one of the effective diabetes mellitus remedy.

Dry them, grind them and make them all into powder form. Mix these powders. Take one teaspoonful of this powder once in the morning, once in the afternoon and then once in the evening. This is one of the surest ways to bring the level of blood sugar in level and to in fact seek a permanent remedy from the condition of diabetes mellitus. This is one of the useful natural remedy for diabetes mellitus.

Guidelines to Control Blood Sugar Levels in Diabetes Mellitus

The whole purpose of diabetes mellitus treatment is to keep the values of blood sugar in check. Apart from the home remedies given above, there are some very significant things you can do to keep the blood sugar levels within safe limits. The following are some guidelines:-

(i) Lead an active lifestyle. Do some active work each day, and in general, try to tire out your body within a particular limit. This will burn down some of the calories in the body.

(ii) Keep strict dietary control. Avoid all sweet and oily foods. Eating sugars is the surest way to increase blood sugar level. Always include at least one bitter dish in each meal.

(iii) Monitor your blood sugar level on a daily basis. Ask your doctor what blood sugar levels are okay for you. Then make attempts to maintain the sugar at that level. Note that if your sugar level goes below this limit, then also it is dangerous, as it can lead to hypoglycemia.

(iv) Take your insulin injections regularly. This will help utilize the excess amount of sugar in the blood.

(v) Do not take a lot of stress. Diabetic people are more vulnerable to cardiovascular diseases, which can be aggravated by stress.

(vi) Keep your body weight in control. Obese people are much more likely to retain higher amounts of sugars in their blood. If you have diabetes, you must seriously consider working out to keep your body weight down.

Gerri Shapiro, MS Ed.

Aloha from beautiful Maui, Hawaii!

My name is Gerri Shapiro, and I'm excited to introduce you to a wonderful pain relief technique that can change your whole life.  It certainly changed mine.

About 25 years ago I lifted a very heavy box.  Immediately I felt something snap in my lower back.  Before I knew what was happening,  I crashed to the floor and couldn't get up.  I could hardly move.  The pain was terrible.

For the next two weeks I could barely get out of bed.. and I could only walk a few steps at a time.  It took many weeks for the pain to finally subside.  Ever since that day, I've suffered from chronic back pain.

Until one day a friend told me about this amazing pain relief technique.  After three sessions, my back pain was HISTORY!   And it has NEVER come back.

I was so excited, I wanted to learn as much as I could about this amazing technique.  And now, I am delighted to share it with YOU.

much Aloha...

Gerri

 

About Gerri

FAQs

 

What is Miracle Pain Relief?

Miracle Pain Relief is a simple, gentle yet powerful technique for relieving pain and stress.  You use your thumbs and fingers to relax muscles,  soothe nerves and bring an ocean of calm and peace to the body.

Where did it come from?

It is based on the "Bowen Technique", which was developed in Australia by Thomas Bowen.

What makes it so amazing?

What makes it so amazing is

how fast it works,

how gentle it feels, and

how long the pain relief lasts

Can Miracle Pain Relief help me?

This technique has helped thousands of people experience great relief from back pain, sciatica, headaches, fibromyalgia, migraine, whiplash, arthritis, sports injuries and much much more.

 

Can anyone learn how to do it?

Yes.  This easy-to-use DVD takes you step-by-step through the process.  It comes with a well-illustrated DVD Photo Guide which simply and clearly illustrates each move. (see below)

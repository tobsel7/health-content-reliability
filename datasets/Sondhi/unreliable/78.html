EDTA

Cardio Restore's liquid formula contains 90% EDTA. EDTA binds or chelates with heavy metals and minerals that are present in the bloodstream and in the walls of blood vessels. Calcium is the main target and is thought to be the biggest contributor to circulatory problems. Too much calcium can cause blood vessels to become calcified and hard, resulting in restricted blood flow. When this occurs, blood cannot be distributed properly to its intended body parts for proper nourishment. As the EDTA dissolves the calcium, it softens the hardened plaque and cholesterol and passes the chelated material out of your body through your urine.

EDTA is recognized by the body as an amino acid, therefore it is allowed to flow freely through-out your entire cardiovascular system. As a result of EDTA's effectiveness at binding to heavy metals and minerals throughout your body, the brain, legs, heart and the rest of your cardiovascular system's circulation improves. EDTA chelation therapy will not only improve and repair small blood vessels, but it will help in promoting the growth of new blood vessels.

L-Arginine

Cardio Restore's liquid formula contains 5% L-Arginine. L-Arginine is a protein amino acid which is found within the proteins of all life forms. Since L-Arginine is a semi-essential amino-acid, the body can synthesize sufficient amounts of the substance to meet its needs. Nitric Oxide is a gas that is produced within the body by L-Arginine. Because of L-Arginine's natural production of Nitric Oxide, this increases the diameter and elasticity of the blood vessels. By improving flexibility in the arteries and relaxing the blood vessels, EDTA chelation can work more effectively. While both Vitamin C Restore and Vitamin E Restore fight free radicals, L-Arginine begins healing your blood vessels from the damage caused by free radical activity. L-Arginine is considered to be one of the most potent nutraceuticals available today. A nutraceutical is any substance that is part of a food, or supplement that provides medical or health benefit, including the prevention and treatment of disease. L-Arginine is referred to by scientists and doctors as the "miracle molecule" because of its powerful healing properties. The properties and benefits of L-Arginine were validated in 1998 with Nobel Prize in Medicine. Due to its effectiveness in healing the cardiovascular system, Columbia University refers to L-Arginine as the "magic bullet".

N-Acetyl Cysteine

Cardio Restore's liquid formula contains 5% N-Acetyl Cysteine. NAC is an altered form of the amino acid Cystein, which is frequently found in foods and synthesized by the body. The primary function of NAC is in the production of glutathione. Glutathione is a small protein found inside cells that play an important role in the body's antioxidant defense system. NAC is metabolized into glutathione once it enters the body. Glutathione then functions as a powerful antioxidant and cell detoxifier. As an antioxidant, glutathione effectively scavenges free radicals, neutralizing their damaging effects on cells and blood vessel walls. As a detoxifier, glutathione can alter chemicals so that they become less harmful to the organs. Glutathione also acts as a detoxifier by removing heavy metals such as lead, mercury and calcium from the body. NAC can also boost immune system function and specifically, may help protect the lung tissues, help people with kidney damage and promote the health of the liver. As of 2006, deficiencies of NAC have not yet been identified and may not exist.
